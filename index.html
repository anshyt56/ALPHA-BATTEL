FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I participate in tournaments?

+

Simply download the app, create an account, and browse the available tournaments. Join any tournament that interests you by paying the entry fee (if applicable) and then compete at the scheduled time.

How are prizes distributed?

+

Prizes are automatically distributed to winners within 24 hours after a tournament ends. You can withdraw your winnings to your bank account, PayPal, or other supported payment methods.

Is there an age requirement?

+

Yes, you must be at least 18 years old to participate in cash tournaments. For non-cash tournaments, the minimum age is 13 with parental consent.
